Welcome to my Retro Web Desktop Project.

A digital recreation inspired by the aesthetics of Macintosh System 7 (1991).

What’s included:
• Windowed interfaces, icons, and UI controls
• Classic title bars, draggable and resizable windows
• Pixel-perfect design with black & white and color variants
• Styled folders, dialog boxes, and text areas
• Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript (no frameworks)
